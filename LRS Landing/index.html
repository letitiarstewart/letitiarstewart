About


Hi! My name is Letitia Stewart. My first love is Critical Care. I am a dual certified Nurse Practitioner in the areas of Family and Acute Gerontology Acute Care. I am currently the lead Critical Care Nurse Practitioner at one of the larger hospitals in Southwest Georgia. For the past two and a half years, I’ve assisted in establishing the critical care program at my hospital. We have three different ICU’s at our facility. As "Lead Nurse Practitioner" , I am an advocate for the Advanced Practice Provider,the Nurse Practitioner and the Physician Assistants teams. There,I assist in helping them define roles, encourage training, and scheduling. Also, I assist in creating a bridge between nurses, APPs, doctors, and hospital administration. Let's work together to see your goals materialized!
